# Meet the Community Board: Share Your Quilts with the World

Quilting has always been a community craft. From quilting bees to guild meetings to online forums, quilters share patterns, techniques, and encouragement. Today we are excited to introduce the QuiltCorgi Community Board — a dedicated space for sharing your digital quilt designs with fellow quilters around the world.

## What Is the Community Board?

The Community Board is a gallery of quilt designs shared by QuiltCorgi users. It lives at [quiltcorgi.com/community](/community) and is accessible to everyone — you do not even need an account to browse.

When you share a design, QuiltCorgi generates a high-quality preview image of your quilt and publishes it to the board with your title, description, and tags. The original project files remain private — only the visual preview is shared.

## How to Share

Sharing a design takes about 30 seconds:

1. Open your project in the studio
2. Click the **Share** button in the top bar
3. Add a title, description, and tags
4. Click **Share to Community**

That is it. Your design appears on the Community Board immediately, where other quilters can see it, like it, and leave comments.

## Browsing and Discovery

The Community Board is designed for leisurely browsing and serendipitous discovery:

### Masonry Layout

Designs display in a responsive masonry grid that adapts to your screen size. The varying preview aspect ratios create a dynamic, engaging visual layout — similar to Pinterest but focused entirely on quilts.

### Search and Sort

Find specific designs by searching titles, descriptions, and tags. Sort by newest or most liked to surface fresh content or community favorites.

### Tags

Tags help categorize designs by style ("modern," "traditional," "art quilt"), technique ("applique," "foundation pieced"), theme ("holiday," "baby," "wedding"), and more. Click any tag to filter the board.

## Interacting with the Community

### Likes

Click the heart icon to like a design. Likes serve two purposes: they let creators know their work is appreciated, and they help surface the best designs for other browsers.

### Comments

Open a design's detail view to leave a comment. We encourage constructive, specific feedback:

- What drew your eye to the design?
- What color or fabric choices stand out?
- How would you adapt the design for your own project?

Comments create conversations that benefit the entire community. A discussion about color theory on one design might inspire a completely different quilter halfway around the world.

### Bookmarks

Save designs to your personal bookmark collection for future reference. This is great for building mood boards, collecting color palette ideas, or saving designs you want to study more closely.

## What People Are Sharing

Even in our beta period, the community has been incredibly creative:

- **Traditional revivalists** who use the block library to recreate vintage quilt patterns with modern fabrics
- **Modern minimalists** designing quilts with just two or three bold solids
- **Pixel art quilters** using the photo patchwork tool to create portraits and landscapes
- **Holiday quilters** sharing seasonal designs that other quilters can adapt
- **Experimental designers** pushing EasyDraw to its limits with complex curved-seam blocks

The diversity of styles is a testament to the flexibility of the tool and the creativity of the community.

## Community Guidelines

We want the Community Board to be welcoming for everyone. Our guidelines are simple:

- **Be kind** — Every design represents someone's creative effort
- **Be constructive** — Feedback should help, not discourage
- **Be original** — Only share designs you created yourself
- **Be respectful** — No spam, no self-promotion in comments, no off-topic content

We moderate the board to maintain a positive environment. Violations are handled with a warning first, because we believe in assuming good intent.

## Privacy and Control

Your creative work, your rules:

- **Unshare anytime** — Remove a design from the board with one click
- **Edit metadata** — Update titles, descriptions, and tags after publishing
- **Project privacy** — Your editable project files are never exposed
- **Custom blocks stay yours** — Custom EasyDraw blocks appear in the preview but are not downloadable

## What Comes Next

The Community Board is the foundation for a richer social experience:

- **Collections** — Curated groups of designs around themes
- **Challenges** — Monthly design challenges with community voting
- **Pattern marketplace** — Sell your QuiltCorgi designs to other quilters
- **Collaborative design** — Invite other quilters to contribute to a shared project

We are building these features based on community feedback, so tell us what matters most to you.

## Join the Community

Whether you are a first-time quilter with your very first design or a 30-year veteran with hundreds of quilts, the Community Board has a place for you. Share your work, discover new inspiration, and connect with quilters who share your passion.

Visit the [Community Board](/community) to start browsing, or [share your first design](/tutorials/community-sharing) today.
