# Introducing QuiltCorgi: Design Quilts in Your Browser

We are thrilled to announce the launch of QuiltCorgi, a modern quilt design tool that runs entirely in your browser. No software to download, no system requirements to check, no license keys to manage. Just open your browser and start designing.

## Why Another Quilt Design Tool?

The quilting world has been underserved by design software for decades. Existing tools are often expensive desktop applications with dated interfaces, steep learning curves, and limited collaboration features. We built QuiltCorgi to change that.

Our goals from day one:

- **Free to start** — No credit card required. The free tier includes the full block library, basic design tools, and up to 3 saved projects.
- **Modern interface** — A clean, intuitive UI built with contemporary design principles. If you can use a modern web app, you can use QuiltCorgi.
- **Cross-platform** — Works on Windows, Mac, Linux, Chromebook, and tablets. Your designs sync across all your devices.
- **Community-first** — Share designs, browse what others are creating, and draw inspiration from a growing community of quilters.

## What You Can Do Today

QuiltCorgi launches with a comprehensive feature set:

### 659+ Quilt Blocks

Our library includes over 659 quilt blocks spanning 20+ categories. Traditional favorites like Ohio Star and Log Cabin sit alongside modern geometric designs, pictorial blocks, holiday themes, art deco patterns, and Celtic knots. Every block is ready to drag into your design and customize with your own colors and fabrics.

### Six Layout Types

Design with grid, sashing, on-point, medallion, and lone star layouts. Add solid or pieced borders, cornerstones, and setting triangles. Each layout type responds to your block choices differently, opening up thousands of possible arrangements.

### EasyDraw Block Drafting

Want a block that does not exist in the library? Draw it yourself with EasyDraw, our seam-line drafting tool. Instead of drawing individual patches, you draw the seam lines that divide a block — and QuiltCorgi automatically determines the resulting patches. It is intuitive for beginners and powerful for experienced designers.

### Real Fabric Visualization

Upload images of your actual fabrics and see them rendered on your quilt design. Calibrate the fabric scale for accurate sizing, and use fussy cutting to position specific motifs on individual patches.

### Precise PDF Export

When your design is ready, export production-quality PDFs with actual-size templates, seam allowances, grain line arrows, and cutting instructions. Foundation paper piecing templates and rotary cutting charts are also available for every project.

## Our Tech Stack

For the technically curious: QuiltCorgi is built with Next.js, React, and Fabric.js for the canvas. The design studio runs entirely client-side for responsive, lag-free interaction. Your projects sync through our API to a PostgreSQL database via Drizzle ORM.

We chose web technologies deliberately. By running in the browser, we can iterate quickly, deploy updates instantly, and reach quilters on any device without the friction of app store approvals.

## Pricing

QuiltCorgi is free to start with generous limits. Our Pro subscription unlocks unlimited projects, unlimited design variations, advanced export options, and priority support. We believe the core design experience should be accessible to everyone.
