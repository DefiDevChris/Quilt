# Community: Sharing Your Designs

QuiltCorgi's community features let you share your designs with other quilters, browse what others have created, and draw inspiration for your next project.

## Sharing a Design

To share one of your quilt designs with the community:

1. Open the project you want to share from your **Dashboard**
2. Click the **Share** button in the top bar (or press `Ctrl+Shift+S`)
3. The Share Dialog appears with options:
   - **Title** — Give your shared design a title (defaults to your project name)
   - **Description** — Write a short description about your design, fabric choices, or inspiration
   - **Tags** — Add tags to help others find your design (e.g., "modern," "traditional," "holiday")
   - **Visibility** — Choose "Community" to share publicly, or "Link Only" for private sharing

4. Click **Share** to publish

Your design appears on the [Community Board](/community) with a rendered preview image. The original project files stay private — only the preview and metadata are shared.

> **Tip:** Designs with descriptions and tags get significantly more views. Take a minute to write about your design process or the story behind it.

## Browsing Community Designs

Visit the [Community Board](/community) to browse designs shared by other QuiltCorgi users. The board offers:

### Search and Filter

- **Search bar** — Find designs by title, description, or tag
- **Sort options** — View newest first or most liked first
- **Layout masonry** — Designs display in a responsive masonry grid that adapts to your screen size

### Design Cards

Each shared design shows:

- A rendered preview of the quilt
- The design title and creator's username
- The date it was shared
- The number of likes and comments
- Tags for quick categorization

## Interacting with Designs

### Liking

Click the heart icon on any design to like it. Liked designs are saved to your **Favorites** list on your profile for easy reference later.

### Commenting

Click a design to open its detail view, then scroll down to the comments section. Leave feedback, ask questions about the design, or share appreciation for the creator's work.

**Community guidelines for comments:**

- Be constructive and kind
- Ask specific questions ("How did you handle the corner triangles?") rather than vague feedback
- Share your own experience if you have tried a similar design

### Saving for Reference

Click the **Bookmark** icon to save any community design to your reference collection without liking it. This is useful for building mood boards or saving designs you want to study later.

## Sharing Outside QuiltCorgi

You can also share your designs beyond the QuiltCorgi community:

### Direct Link

Every shared design gets a unique URL (e.g., `quiltcorgi.com/community/design-abc123`). Copy and paste this link in emails, social media, or quilting forums.

### Social Media

The Share Dialog includes quick-share buttons for popular platforms. Your design's preview image is automatically formatted for each platform's preferred dimensions.

### Export as Image

From the Share Dialog, click **Download Image** to save a high-resolution PNG of your quilt design. Use this in blog posts, pattern listings, or printed materials.

## Privacy and Control

You maintain full control over your shared designs:

- **Unshare** — Remove a design from the community board at any time
- **Edit** — Update the title, description, or tags after sharing
- **Project remains private** — Sharing only publishes the preview, not your editable project files
- **Block original designs** — Custom blocks you created are visible in the preview but not downloadable by others

## Profile and Reputation

Your QuiltCorgi profile displays:

- All your publicly shared designs
- Your total likes received
- Your comment history
- A join date badge

As you share more designs and engage with the community, your profile becomes a portfolio of your quilting journey.
