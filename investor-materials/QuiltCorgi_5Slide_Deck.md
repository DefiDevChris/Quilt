# QuiltCorgi Investor Presentation
## 5-Slide Deck

---

## SLIDE 1: The Problem & The Opportunity

**DESIGN:** Split screen - frustrated quilter imagery left, stats right. Warm cream background.

**THE PROBLEM:**
Quilters today juggle 4+ different tools:
- Electric Quilt 8 ($300, complex, desktop only)
- Facebook Groups (cluttered, not quilt-focused)
- YouTube (one-way learning)
- Instagram (no design tools, algorithm hides posts)

**No single place to design, learn, and connect.**

**THE OPPORTUNITY:**
- **$4.5 billion** quilting industry (US, 2025)
- **9–11 million** active quilters in North America
- Average dedicated quilter spends **$3,200/year** on fabric
- Ages 60+ are traditional base, but under-45 segment growing fast via Instagram/TikTok

**MASCOT:** corgi-14-shaking-head.png (confused by fragmented tools)

## SLIDE 2: The Solution

**DESIGN:** Centered glass card with two sections. Product screenshot/mockup background.

**QuiltCorgi = Design Studio + Community**

**DESIGN STUDIO:**
- 659+ quilt blocks
- 4 worktables (Quilt, Block, Image, Print)
- Automatic yardage calculator
- True-scale PDF patterns with seam allowances
- Photo-to-quilt AI (snap a photo, recreate digitally)

**SOCIAL THREADS:**
- Share designs with quilters worldwide
- Browse inspiration gallery
- Learn from tutorials
- Connect with quilters who share your style

**Why it wins:**
- First platform combining professional design tools + quilt-focused community
- Browser-based (no downloads)
- 1/38th the cost of EQ8 ($8/month vs $300 upfront)

**MASCOT:** corgi-02-jumping.png (excited solution)

**DESIGN:** Two pricing cards side-by-side. Revenue projection chart below.

**CHART:** 12-month revenue projection line graph

**PRO TIER: $8/month or $60/year**

**CONSERVATIVE PROJECTIONS (3% conversion):**
