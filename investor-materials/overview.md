## What It Is

A browser-based quilt design studio with a built-in social community for quilters.

Free to start. Subscription-supported. Nothing like it exists.

## The Market

- **$4.5 billion** U.S. quilting industry (2025)
- **9–11 million active quilters** in the U.S.
- Market projected to reach **$6.7–$7.8 billion globally by 2031**
- **18% of quilters are brand new to the craft** — fresh audience entering every year
- Quilters are passionate, loyal, and spend heavily on their hobby

---

## The Problem

Quilters today have two bad options:

**Desktop software (Electric Quilt 8, BlockBase+)**
- $120–$400 one-time purchase
- Windows/Mac only — no browser, no mobile
- No community, no sharing, no social layer
- Aging design and UX

**Free web tools (PreQuilt, PatternJam)**
- Shallow feature sets
- No yardage calculations, no true-scale PDF export
- No community, no tutorials, no blog

**The gap:** No modern, browser-based design studio exists that also gives quilters a social home — a place to design, share, learn, and connect, all in one place.

---

## What This Platform Does

### The Design Studio
- **659+ quilt block library** — browse or draft your own
- **Six layout modes** — grid, sashing, on-point, medallion, lone star, freeform
- **Automatic yardage & cutting calculations**
- **True 1:1 scale PDF export** — seam allowances built in, ready to print and cut
- **Upload your own fabrics** — see real prints on your design before you buy
- **Photo-to-pattern** — snap a photo of a quilt and recreate it digitally (Pro)
- Works in any browser, on any device — no install required

### Social Threads — The Community
A focused, quilt-only social space. Not Instagram. Not a Facebook group. Built specifically for quilters.

- Share finished quilts and works-in-progress
- Ask questions, get answers, give feedback
- Follow makers you love, build your feed
- Threaded comments and real conversations
- Moderated and welcoming by design

### Learn
- Step-by-step tutorials (beginner through advanced)
- Blog — tips, techniques, pattern spotlights
- Community-submitted content with editorial review

## Revenue Streams

### 1. Subscriptions — Primary
- $8/month or $60/year
- 9M+ quilters × even 1% conversion × $60/yr = **$5.4M ARR**
- At 3% conversion: **$16.2M ARR**

### 2. Fabric Brand Partnerships — Near-Term
- Feature real fabric collections (Andover, Moda, Riley Blake, etc.) inside the design studio
- Quilters design with actual fabrics, then buy them
- Revenue via affiliate commissions, sponsored placement, or direct resale/dropship
- *(Andover Fabrics integration already in development)*

### 3. Equipment Brand Partnerships — Medium-Term
- Sewing machines, cutting machines, longarm quilters, tools
- Affiliate links, sponsored tutorials, co-branded content
- Brands like AccuQuilt, Handi Quilter, Bernina, Brother already market heavily to this audience

### 4. Quilting Educator Partnerships — Medium-Term
- Reputable quilters and teachers host video lessons on the platform
- Drives community growth, SEO, and Pro upgrades
- YouTube is already the #1 quilting education platform — bring that audience home

### 5. Event Promotion — Opportunistic
- Quilt shows, retreats, guild events, online workshops
- Paid listings promoted to the community

### 6. Pattern Marketplace — Future
- Quilters sell their own patterns directly on the platform
- Designs made in the studio, sold here — a natural flywheel
