## SLIDE 1: What Is QuiltCorgi?

**DESIGN:** Split screen. Left: Product screenshot/mockup. Right: Key features.

**A web-based quilt design studio with a built-in community.**

**DESIGN STUDIO:**
- Design quilts in your browser (no software to install)
- 659+ quilt blocks
- 4 worktables: Quilt, Block, Image, Print
- Automatic yardage calculator
- True-scale PDF patterns with seam allowances
- Photo-to-quilt AI feature

**SOCIAL THREADS:**
- Share designs with other quilters
- Browse inspiration gallery
- Learn from tutorials and blog posts
- Connect with quilters worldwide

**Status:** Built and ready for users

## SLIDE 2: The Market

**DESIGN:** Simple stats layout with bar chart.

**INDUSTRY SIZE:**
- $4.5 billion quilting industry (US, 2025)
- 9–11 million active quilters in North America
- Stable market, growing 18% new quilters (up from 11% in 2024)

**QUILTER PROFILE:**
- Average age: 63–65 years (traditional base)
- Growing under-45 segment via Instagram/TikTok
- Dedicated quilters spend $3,200/year on fabric/supplies
- 99 yards of fabric purchased per year

**OPPORTUNITY:** No platform combines professional design tools with a quilt-focused community

## SLIDE 4: How It Works + Revenue Projections

**DESIGN:** Top half: Free vs Pro tiers. Bottom half: Revenue graph.

**FREE TIER:**
- Basic design tools
- 20 starter blocks
- Browse community and tutorials
- Cannot save projects

**PRO TIER ($8/month or $60/year):**
- Save unlimited projects
- Full 659+ block library
- Export patterns (PDF, PNG, SVG)
- Photo-to-quilt AI feature
- Post to community
- Upload custom fabrics

## SLIDE 5: Operations

**DESIGN:** Simple list format. Team structure diagram.

- AWS S3 - Image/file storage

**TEAM NEEDED:**
