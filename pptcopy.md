## Slide 2: The Problem

**Current tools fail quilters.**

| Solution | Problem |
|----------|---------|
| Electric Quilt 8 | $240–300, Windows/Mac only, no community, aging UI |
| Free tools (PreQuilt, PatternJam) | No PDF export, limited blocks, zero community |
| Facebook Groups | Algorithm hides posts, scattered, no design tools |

**The gap:** No platform combines professional design + community in a browser.

---

## Slide 3: The Solution

**QuiltCorgi = Design studio + community, free to start.**

**Core Features:**
- 659+ quilt blocks, 6 layout modes (grid, sashing, on-point, medallion, lone star, free-form)
- Automatic yardage calculations with sub-cutting charts
- True 1:1 scale PDF export with seam allowances
- Photo-to-pattern AI (snap a photo, recreate it digitally)
- "Social Threads" community with threaded comments

**Pricing:**
- **Free:** 20 blocks, 10 fabrics, browse community
- **Pro:** $8/month or $60/year (37% savings) — unlimited everything

## Slide 4: The Business Model

**Multiple revenue streams. Low overhead. Fast path to profit.**

| Stream | Model |
|--------|-------|
| **Primary:** Subscriptions | $8/mo or $60/year |
| **Expansion:** Fabric partnerships | 8–15% affiliate commissions (Andover coded) |
| **Expansion:** Equipment affiliates | Bernina, Brother, AccuQuilt commissions |
| **Future:** Pattern marketplace | Etsy model for quilt patterns |

**Unit Economics (3% conversion):**
- 5,000 users → 150 Pro → $14,400/year
- 50,000 users → 1,500 Pro → $144,000/year
- 100,000 users → 3,000 Pro → $288,000/year

**Break-even:** ~8,000 users | **Profitability:** 12–18 months
